Work

We partner with organizations to audit, remediate, and sustain digital accessibility. Below are examples of engagement structures and deliverables. (Client names and sensitive details are anonymized.)

Engagement types

Baseline audit

Outcome: Comprehensive assessment with prioritized roadmap—severity, affected users, and remediation paths across your product ecosystem.

Remediation sprint

Outcome: Ticket-ready fixes, code patterns, and QA protocols that your team ships in the next sprint or cycle.

Program buildout

Outcome: Definition of done, training, governance, and regression testing—accessibility embedded in your workflow.

What we deliver

  • Findings reports: Severity, affected users, WCAG criterion, repro steps, and remediation guidance.
  • Implementation support: Code snippets, design patterns, and QA acceptance criteria your team can test against.
  • Executive summaries: Risk overview and business impact for stakeholders.
  • Scope: Web applications, websites, mobile apps, and documents (PDF, Word, etc.).
