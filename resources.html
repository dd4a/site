Short list of standards and practical references. Replace/expand as you like.

Standards & guidance

  • WCAG 2.2 (Web Content Accessibility Guidelines)
  • WAI-ARIA Authoring Practices (APG)
  • EN 301 549 (ICT accessibility requirements)

Practical checks I recommend

  • Keyboard-only navigation (Tab/Shift+Tab/Enter/Space/Escape)
  • Headings/landmarks (structure that matches visual layout)
  • Forms (labels, errors, instructions, status messages)
  • Color/contrast (don’t rely on color alone)
  • Motion/animation (support reduced-motion users)
