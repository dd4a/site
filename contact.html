Contact

Email us to start a discussion about your accessibility project. Based in Canada, working worldwide including the US.

What happens next

We typically confirm a short intake call to scope the engagement, provide a written proposal and timeline, and begin a baseline assessment or targeted sprint once agreed. Initial calls are no obligation.

Helpful intake information

To speed up scoping, have the following available where possible:

  • Primary URL(s), platform (web/CMS/framework), and volume of pages or apps.
  • Design system or component library information if available.
  • Current accessibility work or past audit reports (if any).
  • Key timelines, procurement constraints, or compliance requirements (e.g., Section 508, AODA).
