Contact

Email us to discuss your accessibility needs. Based in Canada, working worldwide including the US.

What happens next

After you contact us we typically: (1) confirm a short intake call to scope the engagement, (2) provide a written proposal and estimated timeline, and (3) begin a baseline assessment or targeted sprint once agreed. Initial calls are no obligation.

Helpful intake information

To speed up scoping, please have any of the following available where possible:

  • Primary URL(s), platform (web/CMS/framework), and volume of pages or apps.
  • Design system or component library information if available.
  • Current accessibility work or past audit reports (if any).
  • Key timelines, procurement constraints, or compliance requirements (e.g., Section 508, AODA).
