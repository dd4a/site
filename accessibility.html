Accessibility statement and standards

Digital Design 4 All (DD4A) is committed to digital accessibility and providing equal access to services. We design and test sites, applications, and documents to conform to recognized accessibility standards for organizations in Canada, the US, and internationally.

Standards supported

  • WCAG 2.2 (Web Content Accessibility Guidelines)
  • EN 301 549 (European accessibility requirements)
  • Section 508 (US federal procurement)
  • ADA considerations (Titles II and III)

Testing methodology

We use a mixed-methods approach combining automated scans, manual expert review, and usability testing with people with disabilities. Our reports include test scripts, sampling rationale, and evidence packages to support defensibility.

Known limitations

We document any known limitations and provide guidance for remediation and sustainable accessibility improvements.
