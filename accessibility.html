Accessibility statement

Digital Design 4 All (DD4A) is committed to digital accessibility and providing equal access to our services. We design and test sites, applications, and documents to conform to recognized accessibility standards.

Standards we support

  • WCAG 2.2 (Web Content Accessibility Guidelines)
  • EN 301 549 (European accessibility requirements)
  • Section 508 (US federal procurement)
  • ADA considerations (Titles II and III)

Testing methodology

We use a mixed-methods approach combining automated scans, manual expert review, and assistive-technology testing (screen readers, keyboard navigation). Our reports include test scripts, sampling rationale, and evidence packages to support defensibility.

Known limitations

Some complex or third-party components may not fully meet all success criteria without vendor cooperation. We document known limitations, mitigation strategies, and recommended next steps in every engagement.

Feedback

If you encounter accessibility barriers on this site or in our materials, please email us.
