About Digital Design 4 All

Digital Design 4 All is an independent accessibility consultancy focused on defensible, durable accessibility outcomes for organizations in regulated and high-stakes environments. Our practice is built on standards development experience, enterprise program leadership, and hands-on testing — not just checklists.

The consultant

DD4A is led by a CPWA-certified accessibility professional with doctoral training in human factors. Our work is grounded in:

  • Standards development — active contributor to international accessibility standards bodies, including ISO and ITU working groups focused on ICT accessibility.
  • Enterprise program leadership — led accessibility programs in regulated environments across financial services and public sector organizations.
  • Hands-on assistive technology testing — primary testing with NVDA, JAWS, VoiceOver (macOS and iOS), and TalkBack. Not automated-scan-only work.
  • Defensible practice — engagements are structured to produce auditable, repeatable findings that hold up to procurement and legal review.

Why DD4A

Most accessibility audits produce findings. Fewer produce findings that organizations can actually act on, sustain, and defend. DD4A engagements are scoped and documented to support the full lifecycle — from initial audit through remediation, governance, and ongoing QA.
