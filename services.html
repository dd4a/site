Services

We work with organizations to audit, remediate, and sustain accessibility across web, mobile apps, and documents. Clear scoping, actionable deliverables, and partnership throughout. Based in Canada, we serve clients worldwide including the US.

1) Accessibility audits

  • Scope: Web applications, websites, mobile apps, PDF documents, and digital content.
  • Standards: WCAG 2.2, EN 301 549, and applicable regulations (ACA, AODA, Section 508).
  • Deliverables: Findings with severity, affected users, technical details, and remediation paths.
  • Optional: Assistive technology testing (screen readers, voice control) and regression QA support.

2) Remediation support

  • Scope: Code-level guidance, design-system pattern fixes, and accessibility refactoring.
  • Outcomes: Ticket-ready recommendations, acceptance criteria, and QA protocols your team can execute now.
  • Teaching: We help dev, design, and QA teams understand the "why" so you prevent issues going forward.
  • Coverage: Web components, mobile interfaces, and document publishing workflows.

3) Programs & governance

  • Strategy: Accessibility roadmaps, roles, definitions of done, and risk assessment.
  • Procurement: Requirements language for vendors, tools, and third-party services.
  • Enablement: Training and workshops for product, design, dev, QA, and content teams.
  • Sustainability: We build systems that scale, not one-off audits.
Quick tip: If you need compliance, you usually need both: (1) a reliable baseline assessment, and (2) ongoing testing to catch regressions. We help with both.
