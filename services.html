Services

Engagements are scoped for clarity and shipped with deliverables your team can use immediately. If you’re not sure what you need, start with a short discovery call.

1) Accessibility audits

  • WCAG 2.2 / EN 301 549 aligned review (web apps, sites, documents)
  • Findings with impact statements, repro steps, and recommended fixes
  • Optional: assistive technology verification and regression QA support

2) Remediation support

  • Ticket-ready recommendations, code-level guidance, and acceptance criteria
  • Design-system pattern fixes (forms, modals, tables, navigation, charts)
  • Developer enablement: “how to avoid this next time”

3) Accessibility programs

  • Governance & roles, definitions of done, and risk-based roadmaps
  • Procurement language and accessibility requirements
  • Training for product, design, dev, QA, and content teams
Tip: If you need “compliance,” you usually need two things: (1) a reliable testing method, and (2) a way to keep accessibility from regressing. I can help with both.
