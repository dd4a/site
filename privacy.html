Privacy policy

Digital Design 4 All (DD4A) is committed to your privacy. This site is accessibility-first, secure, and low-data by design.

What we collect

  • Contact information: If you email us directly, we receive and keep your name, email, and message to respond to your inquiry.
  • Site analytics: This site does not use analytics tracking, cookies, or third-party services.
  • Logs: Basic server logs (IP, page viewed, timestamp) may be retained per standard hosting practice.

How we use information

  • To respond to inquiries and provide consulting services.
  • To maintain records required under Canadian business law.
  • We do not sell or monetize personal information. We do not share or use your information for marketing without consent.

Data retention

  • Contact and project information is kept for business and tax purposes and securely archived.
  • You may request deletion of your information at any time (subject to legal or contractual hold requirements).

Security

We use reasonable security measures to protect your information. This site is served over HTTPS (encrypted). If you have concerns, please reach out.
